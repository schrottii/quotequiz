How to Play

1. Click "Start Game" to start a game, containing 20 rounds!
2. In each round, a quote is presented, and 4 buttons of people who might have said it. Only one option is correct.
3. Each round has a time limit of 10 seconds. Trophies received are based on the answer speed, ranging from 1 to 10 (if faster than 1 second)
4. Answering incorrectly or letting the time run out takes the trophies from the question away. Answering slower does not.
Have fun!
